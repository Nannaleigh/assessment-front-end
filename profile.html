Career Goals and Experience:

I have experience in managment and customer service, specializing in de-escalation techniques and problem solving. I also have skills in web-based customer services such as responding professionally to e-mails, chats, and other electronic requests. I have an attention to detail and love the feeling of accomplishment after a job well done.

Recently, I took the leap out of customer service and pursued a passion I have recently discovered: coding. It perfectly combines structure and problem solving with the added bonus of creativity and out of the box thinking. It is my goal to eventually get paid for playing around with words and scripts all day - doing something I love and never truly working a day in my life.
